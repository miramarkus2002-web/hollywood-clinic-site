About

What Is Votiva?

Votiva is an FDA-cleared device that uses radiofrequency energy to gently treat women's intimate health concerns — without surgery, without downtime, and without anesthesia. It works by warming the internal and external tissues to stimulate collagen production, improve tone, and restore comfort.

At Hollywood Clinic, Votiva sessions are performed by certified female specialists in a completely private setting. The procedure is comfortable, quick (typically 30 minutes), and you can return to your daily life immediately — including the next day.

When Votiva Can Help

Votiva addresses common intimate health concerns that most women experience at some point in life — often after childbirth, with age, or hormonal changes.

Vaginal Tightening

Improves internal tone and tightness, often loosened by childbirth, age, or hormonal changes.

Urinary Incontinence

Helps with mild stress urinary incontinence — leakage when laughing, coughing, sneezing, or exercising.

Post-Childbirth Restoration

Restores tissue tone and function after vaginal delivery — a natural, non-invasive recovery option.

Dryness & Discomfort

Improves moisture and comfort, especially during perimenopause and menopause.

Sensitivity & Sensation

Enhances tissue quality and natural sensation, contributing to confidence and intimacy.

External Appearance

Improves the tone and appearance of external intimate skin without surgery or downtime.
