What is Body FX?

Body FX is a non-invasive body contouring device from InMode. The device is manufactured by InMode. It combines three powerful technologies in one device: radiofrequency (RF) heating, negative pressure (vacuum), and high-voltage pulses, all working together to contour the body, improve cellulite appearance, and tighten loose skin. Body FX shapes and refines the area — it is a contouring treatment, not a weight-loss treatment. Unlike surgical or minimally invasive options, Body FX requires no needles, no surgery, no anesthesia, and no downtime — you can walk in, complete a session, and walk out to resume your normal day. The device is FDA-approved and globally recognized as a safe, effective non-invasive contouring platform. Body FX works best as part of a structured series of sessions, where each treatment builds on the last to gradually refine the shape of the area, improve skin texture, and contour the body over time.

The Process

How the session works?

Consultation & assessment — your doctor evaluates your fat pockets, skin laxity, and cellulite, maps the target areas, and sets the right protocol and number of sessions.

Cleansing & prep — the area is cleansed and a thin layer of glide gel is applied so the applicator moves smoothly and the energy is delivered evenly.

Positioning — you lie down comfortably and the Body FX applicator is placed on the first target zone (abdomen, flanks, thighs, arms, or buttocks).

Combined energy — the device delivers radiofrequency heat, vacuum suction, and high-voltage pulses together, while ACE temperature feedback keeps the skin at the ideal therapeutic temperature.

What you feel — a warm, deep suction-massage sensation with brief snap-like pulses. Comfortable and tolerable for most people, with no anesthesia needed.

Even coverage — the applicator is moved in steady, overlapping passes so the whole area is treated uniformly.

Finishing — the gel is removed and soothing skincare is applied. Slight redness may appear for an hour or two but is not visible to others.

Aftercare & plan — you return to work, exercise, or daily life immediately. A typical course is 6–8 sessions, once a week, with results building over the following weeks as collagen rebuilds.

Contraindications

Safety first

Body FX is not suitable in the following cases. A full medical history is always reviewed before treatment at Hollywood Clinic.

  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding
  • Pacemakers or any implanted electronic medical device
  • Metal implants in the treatment area
  • Active skin infection or open wounds in the treatment area
  • Severe uncontrolled diabetes or impaired healing capacity
  • Severe varicose veins in the treatment area
  • Active cancer or recent radiation in the area
  • Severe autoimmune conditions affecting the skin
  • Permanent fillers in the treatment area — Body FX is generally safe with hyaluronic acid fillers, but permanent implants are a contraindication
  • Recent surgery in the area — wait until fully healed
  • Bleeding or clotting disorders
  • A complete medical history is always reviewed before Body FX at Hollywood Clinic

Treatment comparisons

Body FX vs. Other Treatments

Body FX vs. Clatuu Alpha (Cryolipolysis)

Clatuu Alpha freezes and permanently destroys fat cells — strong fat reduction but no skin tightening. Body FX combines RF heating, vacuum, and electrical pulses for gradual fat reduction PLUS skin tightening in one device. Best combined: Clatuu Alpha for serious fat removal, Body FX for refinement and skin tightening.

Body FX vs. Tesla Former / EMSculpt

Tesla Former builds muscle through electromagnetic stimulation. Body FX reduces fat and tightens skin. They address completely different layers and goals — used together they create comprehensive sculpting (Tesla Former for muscle, Body FX for fat and skin).

Body FX vs. EMTONE

EMTONE is purpose-built for cellulite — combining RF with targeted pressure waves. Body FX also improves cellulite, but as one part of a broader contouring effect. For severe cellulite, EMTONE is more focused; for general body refinement with mild cellulite, Body FX is the more versatile choice.

Body FX vs. BodyTite

BodyTite is a minimally invasive procedure (tiny incision, local anesthesia) that liquefies and aspirates fat AND tightens skin — significantly stronger results in one session, but with days of downtime. Body FX is fully non-invasive with no downtime, but works gradually over multiple sessions. Many patients use Body FX for maintenance after a BodyTite procedure.

Body FX vs. Morpheus8 Body

Morpheus8 Body uses RF microneedling for deeper skin remodeling and stronger collagen response — better for stretch marks and significant skin issues, but with a few days of downtime. Body FX is fully surface-based with no downtime — gentler, more about ongoing maintenance and fat reduction.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Body FX hurt?

No. Most patients describe it as a warm massage with a gentle pulling/suction sensation. The high-voltage pulse phases produce a brief, snap-like sensation but are very tolerable. No anesthesia is required.

How long is each session?

From 30 to 60 minutes, according to the area treated and how many zones you do in one visit.

How many sessions do I need?

A typical course is 6–8 sessions, performed once a week. Some patients see early changes after 3–4 sessions; final results usually take the full course plus another 4–6 weeks for collagen rebuilding.

Is there any downtime?

No. You can return to work, exercise, or any normal activity immediately. No side effects, no downtime — slight redness in the treated area may last an hour or two, then fades on its own.

Is Body FX a weight loss treatment?

No. Body FX is a contouring treatment — it reduces fat thickness in treated areas and tightens skin, but it doesn't treat overall body weight. The best candidates are within a reasonable range of their target weight.

Will Body FX help with cellulite?

Yes — Body FX delivers measurable improvement in cellulite appearance over a series of sessions. For severe cellulite cases, EMTONE may be a more focused choice; combining the two often produces the strongest result.

Is Body FX safe for darker skin tones?

Yes. Body FX uses radiofrequency, which doesn't target melanin — making it completely safe across all Fitzpatrick skin types, with no pigmentation risk.

Can I do Body FX after a liposuction or BodyTite procedure?

Yes — Body FX is excellent as a maintenance treatment after liposuction, BodyTite, or a tummy tuck. Your doctor will advise you on the right waiting period before starting (usually 4–6 weeks).

How is Body FX different from generic RF body sculpting?

Body FX is an InMode-engineered device with ACE temperature feedback technology and combined RF + vacuum + high-voltage pulse delivery in one device. Generic 'RF body machines' typically lack one or more of these components, the temperature control, or the clinical safety data. Hollywood Clinic uses only the authentic device.
