About

What are Skin Boosters

Skin Boosters are micro-injections of pure or stabilized hyaluronic acid (and sometimes vitamins, peptides, and antioxidants) placed superficially into the skin. Unlike fillers, they don't reshape or add volume — they hydrate the skin from within, improve its elasticity, smoothness, and natural glow. Think of Skin Boosters as a deep, long-lasting hydration treatment delivered directly to where moisturizers and serums can't reach. They work at the level of the dermis, where they bind water, support collagen, and gradually transform skin quality over a series of sessions. At Hollywood Clinic Egypt, Skin Boosters are used for the face, neck, décolletage, hands, and even acne-scarred or sun-damaged skin — anywhere the skin needs to look healthier, brighter, and more luminous.

Treatment Comparisons

Skin Boosters vs. Other Treatments

Skin Boosters vs. Botox

Botox treats muscles (expression lines). Skin Boosters treat skin quality (hydration, glow, elasticity). They don't compete — they're often combined.

Skin Boosters vs. Filler

Filler reshapes and adds volume. Skin Boosters don't change your shape — they improve the skin itself. A filler patient with dull skin still has dull skin; a Skin Booster patient has glowing skin without any change to features.

Skin Boosters vs. Biostimulators

Skin Boosters mainly hydrate and improve quality with HA. Biostimulators trigger collagen production for structural rejuvenation. Skin Boosters work faster but Biostimulators last longer and address deeper changes.

Skin Boosters vs. Mesotherapy

Mesotherapy is a category of injections that includes Skin Boosters. Some Skin Boosters (like NCTF) are technically advanced mesotherapy cocktails. The difference is in formulation, regulation, and clinical evidence — premium branded Skin Boosters have more research behind them.

Skin Boosters vs. Topical Skincare

Topical skincare works on the skin's surface. Skin Boosters are placed in the dermis, where serums and creams can't reach. They work alongside skincare, not in place of it.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the difference between a Skin Booster and a filler?
A filler reshapes (lips, cheeks, jaw); a Skin Booster doesn't change shape — it improves skin quality (hydration, glow, elasticity). They use similar materials (hyaluronic acid) but with different concentrations and placement depths.
Will Skin Boosters make me look different?
No — that's the beauty of it. Skin Boosters won't change your features. You'll look like yourself, but with healthier, brighter, more hydrated skin.
How many sessions do I need?
Most Skin Boosters require a series of 2–3 sessions, 4 weeks apart, to build the result. Some products (like Profhilo) work with just 2 sessions. Your doctor will design the right plan for you.
How long do results last?
Typically 4 to 9 months, depending on the product, your skin, and your lifestyle. Maintenance sessions every 4–9 months preserve the result.
Does the procedure hurt?
Topical numbing cream is applied beforehand, so most patients describe the discomfort as mild — small pricks at most.
Is there downtime?
Very little. Small injection bumps may be visible for a few hours; mild redness for up to 24 hours. You can wear makeup the next day.
Can I do Skin Boosters before a wedding or event?
Yes — but start at least 4–6 weeks before the event so results have time to develop. Don't book the last session in the same week as the event.
Can Skin Boosters be combined with other treatments?
Yes — they work beautifully alongside Botox, fillers, biostimulators, micro-needling, and LED. Hollywood Clinic specializes in combination treatment plans.
Are Skin Boosters safe?
Yes, when performed by a qualified injector using regulated brands. The products are made from hyaluronic acid, a substance the body produces naturally.
