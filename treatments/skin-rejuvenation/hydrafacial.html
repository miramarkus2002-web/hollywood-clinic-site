About

What Is Hydrafacial

Hydrafacial is a medical-grade, multi-step facial treatment that combines deep cleansing, gentle exfoliation, painless extraction, and intense hydration with infusion of targeted serums — all delivered through a patented vortex-fusion device. Unlike traditional facials, Hydrafacial doesn't rely on harsh scrubbing or aggressive extraction. The device uses controlled suction and a stream of water and active solutions to lift away impurities, dead skin cells, and oil from inside the pores — while simultaneously delivering antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid into the skin. The result is immediately visible: a cleaner, brighter, more hydrated, and noticeably more even-toned complexion — with zero downtime. You can return to work or social events the same hour.

Safety First

Contraindications

  • Hydrafacial is very safe, but should be avoided or postponed in the following cases: Active severe acne, cystic acne, or open lesions on the treatment area
  • Active herpes or cold sore outbreak on the face
  • Severe rosacea flare-ups
  • Sunburn or irritated skin at the time of the session
  • Recent chemical peels, laser treatments, or aggressive resurfacing — wait until the skin is fully healed
  • Known allergy to any of the serum ingredients
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding — only certain serums are pregnancy-safe; this must be reviewed with the doctor in advance
  • A full medical history and skin assessment are always conducted before the session at Hollywood Clinic

Treatment Comparisons

Hydrafacial vs. Other Treatments

Hydrafacial vs. Traditional Facial

Traditional facials rely on manual cleansing, steam, and pressure-based extraction. Hydrafacial is a medical device with controlled suction, no painful squeezing, and serum infusion built into the same session — making it deeper, gentler, and more effective.

Hydrafacial vs. Chemical Peels

Chemical peels exfoliate the skin using acids and can cause peeling, redness, and 3–7 days of downtime. Hydrafacial achieves gentle exfoliation with zero downtime — but for stubborn pigmentation or scarring, a chemical peel can be more aggressive and faster-acting. Many patients combine both.

Hydrafacial vs. Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion uses crystals or a diamond tip to physically exfoliate dry skin. Hydrafacial does the same exfoliation but adds hydration and serum infusion in the same step — making it more comfortable and more multi-functional.

Hydrafacial vs. Forma (Radiofrequency Skin Tightening)

Hydrafacial focuses on skin surface — cleanliness, glow, hydration, texture. Forma works deeper, using radiofrequency to tighten and stimulate collagen. They're not competitors — they're complementary, which is exactly why Hollywood Clinic offers them together as the iFace package.

Hydrafacial vs. Mesotherapy / Skin Boosters

Mesotherapy and Skin Boosters use injections to deliver nutrients deep into the skin and last for months. Hydrafacial is non-injectable and works on the surface — gentler, faster, no needles, but with shorter-lasting glow.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Hydrafacial hurt?
No. Hydrafacial is completely painless. Most patients describe it as a cool, refreshing sensation — there's no scrubbing, no painful squeezing, and no burning.
How long does a Hydrafacial session take?
A standard session takes 30–45 minutes depending on the protocol level and add-ons selected.
Is there any downtime after Hydrafacial?
None. You can apply makeup, go back to work, or attend an event immediately after the session.
Is Hydrafacial safe for sensitive skin?
Yes — Hydrafacial is one of the few medical-grade treatments that's safe for sensitive skin, since the boosters and intensity are fully customized.
Will Hydrafacial clear my acne?
Hydrafacial significantly helps mild to moderate acne and clogged pores by cleansing pores and reducing congestion. For severe or cystic acne, it's best used as part of a wider treatment plan.
Is Hydrafacial safe during pregnancy?
Generally yes — but only certain boosters and active ingredients are pregnancy-safe. The doctor will review your protocol before the session.
How is iFace different from a regular Hydrafacial?
iFace is a Hollywood Clinic combined treatment that pairs Hydrafacial (for surface glow) with Forma (for deeper skin tightening) in one visit — giving you both effects in a single session.
How do I make sure I'm getting an authentic Hydrafacial?
True Hydrafacial™ is a specific patented device. Many clinics in Egypt use imitation devices labeled 'Hydra' or 'AquaPure' that are not equivalent. Always ask the clinic. At Hollywood Clinic, we use the original Hydrafacial™ device and original boosters.
