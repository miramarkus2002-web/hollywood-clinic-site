About

Hospital Expertise Meets Personalized Care

Dr. Ehssan Rabie is a Clinical Nutrition Specialist at Hollywood Clinic Egypt, specialized in non-surgical body reshaping, weight management, and cellulite treatment. She brings more than 10 years of clinical experience in this field — along with 13 years of hospital experience at Kasr Al-Ainy Hospital, one of Egypt's most prestigious medical institutions.

She holds a Master's degree from Cairo University and is currently a PhD candidate, reflecting her strong academic background and her commitment to an evidence-based medical practice. This combination of deep clinical hospital experience and continuous academic research is what defines her approach to every case.

At Hollywood Clinic, Dr. Ehssan combines solid scientific knowledge with extensive hands-on experience to deliver safe, effective, and fully personalized body reshaping and clinical nutrition programs — designed around each patient's body composition, medical history, and goals.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dr. Ehssan Rabie?

Dr. Ehssan Rabie is a Clinical Nutrition Specialist at Hollywood Clinic Egypt, specialized in non-surgical body reshaping and weight management. She holds an MSc from Cairo University, is currently a PhD candidate, and has 13 years of hospital experience at Kasr Al-Ainy.

What treatments does Dr. Ehssan provide?

Non-surgical body contouring and fat reduction, cellulite and body laxity management, medical weight loss programs, clinical nutrition and lifestyle modification, and body composition and metabolic assessment.

Does Dr. Ehssan perform surgical procedures?

No. Dr. Ehssan's entire practice is non-surgical, combining advanced reshaping technologies with personalized clinical nutrition plans.

How are her programs different from regular diet plans?

Her programs are designed medically, based on objective body composition and metabolic assessment, not generic templates — which is why they're suitable even for complex cases and produce more sustainable results.

Where can I book a session with Dr. Ehssan?

Sessions with Dr. Ehssan are available at Hollywood Clinic in Egypt. Booking is done by phone or WhatsApp directly with the clinic.

Are the programs suitable for patients with medical conditions?

Yes. With her hospital background at Kasr Al-Ainy, Dr. Ehssan designs plans that take into account each patient's medical history, current health status, and individual needs.
