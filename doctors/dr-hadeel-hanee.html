About

A Surgeon of Rare Expertise

Dr. Hadeel Hanee is a Consultant in Plastic, Burn and Maxillofacial Surgery at Hollywood Clinic Egypt — one of the most accomplished female plastic surgeons in the Egyptian market, with a rare combination of academic, international, and humanitarian surgical experience.

She is a Lecturer of Plastic Surgery at Ain Shams University, a Fellow of Plastic and Maxillofacial Surgery at the University of Maryland, Baltimore — specifically at the world-renowned Shock Trauma Center — and a Specialist of Surgical Oncology at Baheya Foundation for the Early Detection and Treatment of Breast Cancer in Egypt.

With over 10 years of surgical practice, Dr. Hadeel has participated in more than 3,500 aesthetic and reconstructive surgery cases, alongside 400+ cases of breast cancer, oncoplastic, and breast reconstruction surgery — a depth of clinical exposure that very few surgeons in Egypt can match.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dr. Hadeel Hanee?

Dr. Hadeel Hanee is a Consultant in Plastic, Burn and Maxillofacial Surgery at Hollywood Clinic Egypt, Lecturer of Plastic Surgery at Ain Shams University, Fellow of the University of Maryland (Shock Trauma Center), and Specialist of Surgical Oncology at Baheya Foundation, with 10+ years of experience and 3,500+ surgical cases.

What types of surgery does Dr. Hadeel perform?

She performs aesthetic (cosmetic) plastic surgery, reconstructive surgery, burn surgery, maxillofacial surgery, and breast cancer / oncoplastic / breast reconstruction surgery.

Does Dr. Hadeel only do cosmetic surgery?

No. Her practice combines aesthetic surgery with reconstructive, trauma, burn, and oncological surgery — which is a rare combination, and it gives her aesthetic cases a much deeper anatomical and safety foundation.

Is Dr. Hadeel qualified to handle breast cancer cases?

Yes. She is a Specialist of Surgical Oncology at Baheya Foundation — Egypt's most respected breast cancer center — and has personally handled 400+ breast cancer, oncoplastic, and breast reconstruction cases.

Where did Dr. Hadeel train internationally?

She holds a fellowship in Plastic and Maxillofacial Surgery at the University of Maryland, Baltimore, including the Shock Trauma Center — one of the world's leading trauma and reconstructive surgery institutions.

Where can I book a consultation with Dr. Hadeel?

Consultations with Dr. Hadeel are available at Hollywood Clinic in Egypt. Booking is done by phone or WhatsApp directly with the clinic.

Will Dr. Hadeel recommend surgery even if it's not necessary?

No. Her philosophy is to recommend surgery only when it's genuinely the right answer for the patient — and to be equally honest when a non-surgical or alternative path is the wiser choice.
