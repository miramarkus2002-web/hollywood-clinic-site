About

Where Nutrition Meets Body Sculpting

Dr. Rana Ibrahim Sultan is a Clinical Nutritionist and Non-Invasive Aesthetic Specialist at Hollywood Clinic Egypt, with a unique professional background that combines physical therapy, clinical nutrition, and advanced non-surgical body sculpting. She has been part of the Hollywood Clinic team since November 2019, with over 8 years of total experience in the field.

Dr. Rana holds a Bachelor's degree from the Faculty of Physical Therapy at Cairo University, followed by a Master's degree from the Burn Surgery Department of the same faculty — a strong scientific foundation that gives her exceptional insight into skin behaviour, tissue healing, and body response to advanced technologies.

She has expanded her specialization through multiple international diplomas in clinical nutrition, breast cancer patient nutrition and rehabilitation, quality and infection control, and the latest non-invasive aesthetic modalities — including a diploma from Wageningen University in the Netherlands and a specialized program at Wesal Institute, Dubai.
