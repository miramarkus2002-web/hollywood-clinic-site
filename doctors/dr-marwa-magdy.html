About

Refined Injection. Combination Mastery.

Dr. Marwa Magdy is a Consultant in Dermatology, Venereology, and Non-Surgical Aesthetic Medicine at Hollywood Clinic Egypt — known across the Egyptian market as one of the most refined advanced injectors and combination treatment specialists. She combines strong medical dermatology training with refined aesthetic expertise to design personalized treatment plans that enhance natural beauty while preserving each patient's unique features.

She earned her Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery in 2015, followed by a Master's degree in Dermatology, Venereology & Aesthetic Medicine from Ain Shams University Hospitals, where she also completed three years of residency. This solid medical foundation is what allows her to approach every aesthetic case with a real understanding of skin health, safety, and long-term outcomes.

Dr. Marwa later chose to dedicate herself fully to aesthetic medicine — not to follow trends, but to elevate cosmetic standards through a strong dermatological background and the most advanced non-surgical techniques. Her work blends scientific precision with an artistic sensibility, delivering elegant, balanced, and natural-looking results.
