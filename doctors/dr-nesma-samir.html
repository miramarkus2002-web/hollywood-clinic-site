About

Laser Dermatology as Science and Art

Dr. Nesma Samir is a Laser Aesthetic Dermatology Specialist at Hollywood Clinic Egypt, with 12 years of dedicated experience in aesthetic laser treatments and non-invasive dermatology. She has built her reputation on a single, consistent principle: natural beauty without exaggeration, and skin transformation without facial distortion.

She earned her Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery (MBBCh) from the Faculty of Medicine at Kasr Al-Ainy, Cairo University — one of Egypt's most respected medical schools — followed by a Diploma in Dermatology & Venereology from Al-Azhar University, and a Diploma in Laser Science Applications from Cairo University. This combined background of clinical dermatology and specialized laser training is what allows her to handle even the most sensitive skin cases with confidence.

At Hollywood Clinic, Dr. Nesma combines diverse advanced technologies and techniques — including injectables and laser-based treatments — to deliver longer-lasting, natural-looking results, with deep attention to every detail of each case.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dr. Nesma Samir?

Dr. Nesma Samir is a Laser Aesthetic Dermatology Specialist at Hollywood Clinic Egypt. She holds an MBBCh from Kasr Al-Ainy, a Dermatology Diploma from Al-Azhar University, and a Laser Sciences Diploma from Cairo University, with 12 years of specialized experience and 200+ documented cases.

What conditions does Dr. Nesma treat?

She treats acne, post-acne marks, acne scars, pigmentation disorders, melasma, wide pores, signs of aging, photoaging (sun damage), and provides laser hair removal and non-surgical skin tightening.

Does Dr. Nesma perform surgical procedures?

No. Her practice is fully non-surgical, focused on laser, advanced dermatology techniques, and injectables for natural, longer-lasting results.

Are her treatments suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. Each case is assessed individually, and the right technology and protocol are selected based on skin type and condition — which is essential for sensitive skin and cases like melasma.

Will the results look natural?

Yes — that's central to Dr. Nesma's vision. She is committed to natural-looking results, with no facial distortion and no overdone appearance, focusing on enhancing rather than changing the patient's identity.

Where can I book a session with Dr. Nesma?

Sessions with Dr. Nesma are available at Hollywood Clinic in Egypt. Booking is done by phone or WhatsApp directly with the clinic.
