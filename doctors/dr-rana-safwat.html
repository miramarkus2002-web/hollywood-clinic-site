About

A Leader in Dermatology & Aesthetic Medicine

Dr. Rana Safwat Mustapha is a leading specialist in dermatology, cosmetic laser, and non-surgical aesthetic medicine at Hollywood Clinic. With more than 12 years of clinical experience, she combines deep medical expertise in dermatology with one of the widest aesthetic skill sets in the market — covering injectables, lasers, advanced skin treatments, and non-surgical gynecological aesthetics.

She earned her MBBCh and Master's degree in Dermatology & Venereology from Ain Shams University, followed by a diploma in Laser Sciences Applications from the National Institute of Laser Enhanced Sciences (NILES) at Cairo University. Her clinical training was completed at Al-Demerdash Hospital, with additional certifications in quality, infection control, and continuous medical education.

Dr. Rana is an active member of the Egyptian Women's Dermatologic Society and the Egyptian Society of Dermatology & Venereology, and she is currently pursuing her doctorate in dermatology with the goal of joining the Canadian Dermatology Association.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Dr. Rana Safwat?

Dr. Rana Safwat Mustapha is a Dermatology, Laser & Non-Surgical Aesthetic specialist at Hollywood Clinic, with an MSc in Dermatology & Venereology from Ain Shams University and over 12 years of experience.

What treatments does Dr. Rana specialize in?

Dr. Rana specializes in Botox, dermal fillers, threads lifting, PRP, mesotherapy, lipolysis, microneedling, chemical peels, hydrafacial, full laser dermatology, acne and pigmentation treatment, hair restoration, and non-surgical gynecological aesthetics (Votiva, Fotona, Morpheus).

Where can I book a session with Dr. Rana?

Sessions with Dr. Rana are available at Hollywood Clinic. Booking is done by phone or WhatsApp directly with the clinic.

Does Dr. Rana perform surgical procedures?

No. Dr. Rana focuses exclusively on non-surgical dermatology, aesthetic, and laser treatments — including non-surgical gynecological aesthetics.

Are the treatments safe?

Yes. Dr. Rana relies only on FDA-grade, scientifically approved technologies, performs a full medical assessment before every plan, and prioritizes natural, non-exaggerated results.

Does Dr. Rana treat both medical and cosmetic skin issues?

Yes. Her practice covers medical dermatology (acne, pigmentation, hair disorders, scars, skin biopsies) alongside cosmetic dermatology and laser, which makes her ideal for combined treatment plans.
