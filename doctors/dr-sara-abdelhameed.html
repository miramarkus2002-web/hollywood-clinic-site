About

A Trusted Name in Body Contouring

Dr. Sara Abdelhameed is one of the most trusted names in non-surgical body contouring and body sculpting in Egypt. As a Clinical Nutrition Consultant from Cairo University with more than 12 years of hands-on experience, she has built her reputation on real, measurable results — not promises.

At Hollywood Clinic Egypt, Dr. Sara designs fully personalized treatment plans for every patient, combining the most advanced global body sculpting technologies with her deep understanding of nutrition, body composition, and skin behaviour. The goal is always the same: a slimmer, firmer, more harmonious body shape — without surgery, without downtime, and with safety as the first priority.

Over the past 12 years, Dr. Sara has personally supervised the treatment of more than 50,000 women in Egypt and the region, helping them eliminate cellulite, melt localized fat, tighten loose skin, and reshape their bodies with consistently high satisfaction rates.
