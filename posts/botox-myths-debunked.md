Botox has been around for decades, yet the same myths keep circulating. Today we're setting the record straight.

## Myth 1: "Botox Freezes Your Face"

This is the #1 fear we hear. The truth: only **bad Botox** freezes your face. When administered by an experienced specialist who understands facial anatomy, Botox softens dynamic wrinkles while preserving your natural expressions. You'll still smile, frown, and laugh — just without the deep creases.

## Myth 2: "It's Toxic"

Botox is a purified protein derived from botulinum toxin. In the microscopic doses used cosmetically, it's been studied for over 30 years and approved by the FDA, EMA, and Egyptian Ministry of Health.

The dose makes the poison. Water can kill you if you drink enough of it. Botox is safer than most over-the-counter medications.

## Myth 3: "Once You Start, You Can Never Stop"

There is zero medical dependency. Botox doesn't change your skin permanently. If you stop, your wrinkles simply return to their pre-treatment state — they don't get worse.

What's true is that many clients **choose** to continue because they love the results.

## Myth 4: "It's Only for Older Women"

Preventive Botox in your late 20s and early 30s actually stops wrinkles from forming in the first place. We see clients ranging from 25 to 75. Age is not the qualifier — visible dynamic lines are.

## Myth 5: "All Botox Is the Same"

Brand matters. We exclusively use **Allergan's original Botox** and **Dysport** — both gold-standard products. Cheaper "Botox" from unregulated sources can be diluted, contaminated, or counterfeit.

## The Bottom Line

Botox, in skilled hands with quality product, is one of the safest and most predictable aesthetic treatments available today.
