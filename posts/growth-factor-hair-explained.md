## The Problem With Hair Vitamins

You take biotin daily. You eat well. You use the expensive shampoo. And your hair still feels thin, dull, and weak.

Here's what nobody tells you: most of the nutrients you swallow as oral hair supplements lose 70% or more of their potency through digestion. By the time anything reaches your hair follicles via your bloodstream, the dose is too diluted to make a meaningful difference. The topical products are even worse — they barely penetrate past the scalp surface.

This is exactly the problem Growth Factor scalp injections solve.

## What Growth Factor Injections Actually Deliver

A Growth Factor session places a pharmaceutical-grade cocktail directly into your scalp, where your hair follicles actually live. The formulation includes:

- **B-complex vitamins** (including biotin, B5, B12) for keratin production
- **Amino acids** like cysteine and methionine — the building blocks of hair protein
- **Minerals** including zinc, copper, and magnesium for follicle metabolism
- **Signaling peptides** that extend the active growth phase
- **Circulation enhancers** to oxygenate the follicle environment

Every ingredient bypasses the digestive system entirely. The dose at the follicle is hundreds of times more concentrated than what any oral supplement can deliver.

## Who Benefits Most

Growth Factor is one of the most versatile treatments in modern aesthetic medicine because it addresses the most common cause of hair problems — undernourished follicles. We see particularly strong results in:

**Post-pregnancy hair shedding.** The postpartum period drains the body's reserves, and hair is often the first thing to suffer. Growth Factor restores what was lost.

**Stress and lifestyle-related thinning.** When sleep, diet, or stress disrupt your normal biology, follicles shrink. Growth Factor reverses this faster than waiting for lifestyle changes to take effect on their own.

**Weak, brittle, dull hair quality.** Even when shedding isn't the main issue, hair that feels thin or lifeless almost always responds dramatically to nourishment delivered at the source.

**Maintenance between bigger treatments.** Patients on Exosome or PRP protocols use Growth Factor between sessions to keep the follicles fed and supported.

## How a Session Works

A typical Growth Factor session takes about 30 minutes:

1. The scalp is cleansed
2. Topical numbing cream is applied (10 minutes)
3. The Growth Factor cocktail is prepared from a sterile single-use vial
4. Fine-needle injections are placed in a grid pattern across the target zones
5. Aftercare instructions: no washing or sweating for 24 hours

Most patients say the injections feel like tiny pricks — uncomfortable but quick. There's no real downtime. Slight redness or pinpoint marks may appear for a few hours.

## The Realistic Timeline

Hair grows on biology's clock, not yours. With a standard 4–6 session course spaced 2–3 weeks apart, expect:

- **Weeks 2–4:** Reduced shedding becomes noticeable
- **Weeks 6–8:** New, thinner "baby hairs" start appearing along the hairline and part
- **Months 3–4:** Improved density and stronger hair quality
- **Months 4–6:** Full results visible

After the initial course, monthly or bi-monthly maintenance sessions keep results sustained.

## Growth Factor vs. Mesotherapy — Is There a Difference?

In everyday market use, these two terms have blurred together. Strictly speaking:

- **Mesotherapy** is the *technique* — small superficial injections in a grid pattern
- **Growth Factor** refers to the *formulation* — typically more concentrated and containing peptide signaling compounds beyond basic vitamins

What actually matters isn't the name. What matters is whether the clinic is using an authentic pharmaceutical-grade product (which we always do at Hollywood Clinic) or a compounded mixture of unknown origin (which we never do).

## Growth Factor vs. Exosomes — Which Should You Choose?

The simplest way to think about it:

**Growth Factor feeds the follicle. Exosomes reprogram it.**

If your hair loss is from a temporary cause — stress, post-pregnancy, nutritional deficit — Growth Factor often produces excellent results on its own. If your hair loss is hereditary or chronic, Exosomes give a deeper, longer-lasting transformation, with Growth Factor as the supportive maintenance treatment between sessions.

Many of our patients combine both in a structured plan. Your specialist will recommend the right protocol after a scalp assessment.
