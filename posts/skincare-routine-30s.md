Your 30s are a turning point for skin. Collagen production drops by roughly 1% each year after age 25. The good news? A smart routine started now can keep you looking radiant for decades.

## Morning Routine (5 Minutes)

1. **Gentle cleanser** — Cream or gel formula, never soap
2. **Vitamin C serum** — Brightens, protects against pollution
3. **Hyaluronic acid** — Plumps and hydrates
4. **Moisturizer** — Light in summer, richer in winter
5. **SPF 50** — The single most important anti-aging step. Non-negotiable.

## Evening Routine (8 Minutes)

1. **Double cleanse** — Oil cleanser first, then water-based
2. **Retinoid** — Start with retinol 2–3 nights per week
3. **Eye cream** — Peptides and caffeine
4. **Night moisturizer** — Slightly richer than morning

## Treatments to Add Quarterly

Even the best home routine has limits. Visit a clinic every 3–4 months for:

- **Hydrafacial** — Deep cleansing and hydration
- **Mesotherapy** — Vitamin cocktails injected into the skin
- **Chemical peels** — Renew the outer layer

## What to Avoid

- Harsh scrubs (use chemical exfoliants instead)
- DIY at-home peels
- Sleeping in makeup
- Skipping sunscreen on cloudy days

## A Word on Botox and Fillers

In your 30s, preventive Botox in expression areas can stop wrinkles from forming. Subtle fillers in the cheeks help maintain youthful structure as fat pads start to descend.

This isn't about looking different. It's about aging on your terms.

## The Most Important Habit

Drink water, sleep 7+ hours, and protect your skin from the sun. Everything else is optimization.
