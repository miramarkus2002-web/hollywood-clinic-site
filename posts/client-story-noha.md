Noha came to us six months ago. After two pregnancies and years of frustration with stubborn fat, she was ready to try something — but she didn't want surgery.

In this honest 6-minute video, Noha shares:

- Why she chose non-invasive body shaping
- What the six sessions actually felt like
- Her real results (she lost 18cm around her waist)
- Whether she'd recommend it

## Her Treatment Plan

- **Sessions:** 6 sessions of cryolipolysis + radiofrequency
- **Duration:** 12 weeks
- **Downtime:** Zero
- **Pain level:** "Like a cold pinch, completely tolerable"

## Real Results, Real Time

Body shaping isn't instant. Final results emerge between weeks 8 and 16 as your body naturally processes the treated fat cells. Noha's video is filmed at the 6-month mark — her final, settled result.

## Could This Work For You?

Body shaping is for clients within 20% of their goal weight who want to target specific areas. It's not weight loss. It's contouring.
