## Why "Natural" Matters More Than Ever

The era of overfilled, duck-shaped lips is over. In 2026, the most requested aesthetic in our Cairo clinic is **subtle volume** — fillers so well-placed that even your closest friends won't be able to tell.

This guide walks you through everything you need to know before your first appointment.

## How Hyaluronic Acid Works

Hyaluronic acid (HA) is a sugar molecule that already exists naturally in your body. When injected into the lips, it:

- Attracts water, creating volume
- Smooths fine vertical lines
- Defines the lip border (vermilion)
- Restores symmetry

The best part? It's fully reversible with an enzyme called hyaluronidase, which means your specialist can dissolve it within minutes if you ever change your mind.

## What to Expect

**Day 0:** 30-minute appointment. Numbing cream, then injections.

**Days 1–3:** Mild swelling. Bruising in 20% of cases. You can return to work the next day.

**Day 7:** Final result settles. This is the look you'll have for 9–12 months.

## Aftercare Essentials

- No exercise for 24 hours
- No alcohol for 48 hours
- No facials or saunas for one week
- Sleep slightly elevated for 2 nights
- Drink lots of water — it helps the HA integrate

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

The biggest mistake we see is patients chasing trends instead of working with their face. A trout pout that looks great on one face can look bizarre on another. Trust an experienced injector who understands your anatomy.
